Bihar Cultural Heritage

Bihar has a rich history of art, culture, and craftsmanship. From ancient traditions to vibrant handicrafts, the state reflects centuries of heritage passed from generation to generation.

Madhubani Painting

Madhubani painting originated in the Mithila region of Bihar. These paintings are known for their bright colors, intricate patterns, and cultural themes inspired by mythology and nature.

Sikki Grass Craft

Sikki grass craft is a traditional eco-friendly craft of Bihar. Artisans create decorative baskets, boxes and toys using golden colored sikki grass.

Terracotta Art

Terracotta craft of Bihar involves shaping clay into beautiful decorative items and sculptures. It represents the ancient pottery traditions of the region.

Tikuli Art

Tikuli art is a unique painting style from Patna. Originally used for decorating bindis, it later evolved into beautiful paintings on wood and glass.

Meet the Artisans

Behind every beautiful craft of Bihar are skilled artisans who keep these traditions alive. Their knowledge and creativity have been passed down through generations, making each piece unique and meaningful.

Mithila Artists

Women artists from the Mithila region create the famous Madhubani paintings using natural colors and traditional techniques.

Sikki Craft Makers

Artisans carefully weave golden sikki grass to create baskets, decorative items and eco-friendly handicrafts.

Terracotta Potters

Traditional potters shape clay into beautiful terracotta decorations and sculptures that reflect Bihar's ancient culture.
